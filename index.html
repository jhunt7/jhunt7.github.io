CV

I am a second year graduate student in the school of mathematical and statistical sciences (SMSS) at Clemson University. My research interests include number theory, L-functions, modular forms, partitions, and combinatorics. My advisor is TBA.

In Spring 2023, I graduated from Vanderbilt University with a B.A. in Mathematics with honors and a minor in computer science. I was advised by Larry Rolen and awarded highest honors on the successful defense of my senior thesis. From 2017-2019, I attended the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics (NCSSM). While attending NCSSM, I particpated in multiple sessions of their research in mathematics (RMath) program.

When not doing math, I enjoy singing and acting, as well as hiking, thrifting, and collecting vinyl records.
